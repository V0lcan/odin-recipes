Description

A cheap meal for students or depressed people!

Ingredients:

Serves 1

  • Ramen (1 package)
  • Water

Instructions:

  1. Remove the ramen from the package and put it in a bowl.
  2. Add water to the bowl and put it in the microwave on high for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add spice from the ramen package.
  4. Enjoy!
